# 2025 BMW 3 Series — Technical Specifications

## Engine Options
1. 330i: 2.0L TwinPower Turbo 4-cylinder, 255 hp, 295 lb-ft
2. 330e: 2.0L Plug-in Hybrid, 288 hp (combined), 310 lb-ft
3. M340i: 3.0L TwinPower Turbo 6-cylinder, 382 hp, 369 lb-ft

## Fuel Economy
- 330i: 25/34 MPG (city/hwy), 28 MPG combined
- 330e: 75 MPGe (electric + gas combined), 22 miles electric range
- M340i: 23/32 MPG (city/hwy), 26 MPG combined

## Dimensions
- Length: 185.7 in
- Width: 71.9 in
- Height: 56.8 in
- Wheelbase: 112.2 in

## Technology
- BMW Curved Display (12.3" + 14.9")
- iDrive 8.5 Operating System
- Harman Kardon Surround Sound (available)
- Wireless Device Charging
- BMW Digital Key Plus

## Safety Features
- Active Driving Assistant (standard)
- Frontal Collision Warning with City Collision Mitigation
- Lane Departure Warning
- Active Blind Spot Detection
- Parking Assistant Plus (available)

## Warranty
- Basic: 4 years / 50,000 miles
- Powertrain: 4 years / 50,000 miles
- Corrosion: 12 years / unlimited miles
- BMW Ultimate Care: 3 years / 36,000 miles (maintenance included)
