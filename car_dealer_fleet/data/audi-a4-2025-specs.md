# 2025 Audi A4 — Technical Specifications

## Engine
- Type: 2.0L TFSI 4-cylinder turbocharged
- Horsepower: 261 hp @ 5,000 rpm
- Torque: 273 lb-ft @ 1,600 rpm
- 0-60 mph: 5.2 seconds

## Fuel Economy
- City: 24 MPG
- Highway: 33 MPG
- Combined: 28 MPG

## Dimensions
- Length: 187.5 in
- Width: 72.7 in
- Height: 56.2 in
- Wheelbase: 111.0 in

## Technology
- Audi Virtual Cockpit (12.3-inch digital display)
- MMI Touch Response (10.1-inch touchscreen)
- Bang & Olufsen 3D Premium Sound System (available)
- Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto

## Safety Features
- Audi Pre Sense Basic and City (standard)
- Adaptive Cruise Control with Stop & Go
- Lane Departure Warning
- Top View Camera System (available)

## Warranty
- Basic: 4 years / 50,000 miles
- Powertrain: 4 years / 50,000 miles
